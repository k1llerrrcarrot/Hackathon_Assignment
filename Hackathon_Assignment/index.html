Welcome to the World of Fighting Games!

Fighting games are a very old video game genre. When arcades where still around, fighting games where at the height of their popularity. Nowadays, there are not many arcades left and the focus of fighting games has since shifted to the home console and PC markets. With this shift in focus also came an expansion of the types of fighting games that are produced.
This website aims to inform you about these existing sub-genres; how they differ, what they share and what games you can find in them.
