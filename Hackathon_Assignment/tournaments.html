Tournaments

As a genre primarily aimed at 1-on-1 competition, very quickly tournaments started popping up for the genre. Over the years these tournaments have grown rapidly in size and the invention of online play has made online tournaments a reality. There is always a tournament you can join, either physically in your community or in online communities.

Some of the bigger events are streamed and can be watched from the comfort of your own home. It allows players to see the best of the best duke it out and they might be able to pick up some new tricks this way!
